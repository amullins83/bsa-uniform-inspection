#Views

##Front Page
 - What it is
 - Log in

##Registration
 - Scout or Inspector
 - Inspectors need a special invitation code
 - Contact Info

##Inspections
 - Create, edit, and view inspection events

##Inspection Detail
 - Add attendees to inspection event
 - List of scouts to view/edit their inspection reports

##Inspection Report
 - List of rules with their descriptions
 - Controls for editing rule evaluations
 - Total score

##Rules
 - List of all rules with their descriptions
 - Links to images, videos, and more detailed explanations
 - Administrators can click a rule to edit
 - Administrators can add/delete rules
